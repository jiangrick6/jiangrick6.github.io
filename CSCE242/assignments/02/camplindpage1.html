Established in 1935, Camp Lindenmere is a private, co-ed, sleepaway camp located on approximately 200 acres of Pennsylvania's beautiful Poconos Mountains. With multi-session programming and nearly 90 activities to choose from, Lindenmere is a celebration of choice, exploration and individuality.

Stuff to Know

  • Camp: Overnight
  • Age Range: 6-17 year old
  • Max Campers: 400 Campers
  • Prices: $6,595-$12,295
  • Start and End: June-August
