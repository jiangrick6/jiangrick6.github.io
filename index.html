Why Use Our App?

GroceryCut is your smart shopping assistant. Built with React and powered by the ChatGPT API, our web app helps users create personalized grocery lists based on their budget and food preferences. Whether you're planning a $50 grocery run or craving a mix of Chinese and Italian cuisine, GroceryCut generates a custom list tailored to your needs. It takes the guesswork out of shopping, helping you stay within budget while exploring the foods you love. Simple, efficient, and intuitive—GroceryCut makes every grocery trip smarter.
